Snowshoe hare

Snowshoe hare's fur turns white during the winter and rusty brown during the summer.

  • Scientific Name: Lepus americanus
  • Length: 41~52 cm
  • Average Lifespan: 3 years in the wild
  • Habitat: Boreal forests & Upper montane forests

It has the name "snowshoe" because of the large size of its hind feet. Snowshoe hare's feet prevent it from sinking into the snow when it hops and walks. They mainly active at night and do not hibernate.
